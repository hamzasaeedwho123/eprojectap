Entertainment

Live shows, parades, and events

Daily

Merciado Parade

Our flagship daytime parade features floats, dancers, and park characters. Runs twice daily. Check park app for times.

Evening

Starlight Show

Nightly show at the main stage: music, lights, and fireworks. Best viewed from the central plaza. Free with park entry.

Live

Street Performers

Magicians, jugglers, and musicians throughout the park. Schedules posted at zone entrances.

Seasonal

Special Events

Holiday festivals, concert nights, and themed weekends. Follow us on social media and check the events calendar.
